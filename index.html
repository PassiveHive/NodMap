The four phases

Every trip has a rhythm. NodMap follows it.

Most apps stop at the trailhead. NodMap walks the whole arc — from the question you have on Saturday morning to the photo you'll search for next October.

01 / Plan

Ask in plain language.

Tell NodMap what you're looking for. Distance, terrain, time of day, water features — say it the way you'd say it to a friend.

02 / Go

Quiet on the trail.

Calm, only-when-needed alerts. Turn-around time. Off-route detection. No chatter when you're trying to be present.

03 / Remember

Trips that come back to you.

Every walk auto-saved. Photos pinned to where you took them. Search "that overlook with the lone tree" and find it.

04 / Share

The people who care.

Family knows where you are when you want them to. One tap pause. No silent shares. Trust without a leash.

What makes it different

Built around three commitments we won't walk back.

Every product makes promises. Most don't keep them. These are the ones that determined what NodMap is — and what it isn't.

01

Your data stays yours.

Local-first storage. Your trips live on your device. Cloud sync is opt-in and end-to-end encrypted. We can't read your data — and we sell it to no one.

02

Works where signal doesn't.

The map renders, the AI thinks, your tracks save — even when the cell tower is two ridges away. Outdoor apps that require connectivity miss the point.

03

Asks instead of assumes.

When NodMap doesn't know something, it tells you. When it makes a recommendation, it shows you why. No silent confidence. No hallucinated trail conditions.
